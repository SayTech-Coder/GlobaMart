📦 GlobalMart Supply Chain Analytics | Power BI Dashboard

📖 Project Overview

The GlobalMart Supply Chain Analytics project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance, supply chain operations, inventory management, supplier performance, and delivery efficiency.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making for executives and supply chain managers.

---

🎯 Business Problem

GlobalMart required a centralized analytics solution to monitor key business operations and answer critical questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest revenue?
- Which product categories are the most profitable?
- How efficient is the current delivery process?
- Which suppliers contribute the most profit?
- Are inventory levels aligned with customer demand?
- What operational improvements can increase profitability?

---

 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance using interactive KPIs.
- Analyze revenue, profit, and profit margins.
- Evaluate supplier contributions to profitability.
- Track delivery performance and shipping efficiency.
- Identify inventory optimization opportunities.
- Support data-driven strategic decisions through interactive dashboards.

---

 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning and preparation
- Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling and visualization
- Power Query** – Data transformation
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures and calculated columns

 📂 Dashboard Overview

The report consists of two executive dashboards:

 1️⃣ Business Performance Dashboard

Provides an overview of:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue vs Profit by Product Category
- Profit Margin by Product Category
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top 10 Suppliers by Profit Contribution

---

 2️⃣ Supply Chain Operations Dashboard

Focuses on operational efficiency by monitoring:

- On-Time Delivery Rate
- Late Delivery Rate
- Average Shipping Time
- Damaged Goods Rate
- Delivery Performance by Supplier
- Delivery Performance by Shipping Mode
- Inventory Levels vs Order Quantity
- Damaged Goods by Product Category
- Monthly Delivery Performance Trend

 Inventory Analysis

The inventory dashboard compares stock levels with customer demand to identify:

- Overstocked products
- Understocked products
- Inventory imbalances
- Product categories with higher damaged goods rates

This supports more effective inventory planning and replenishment.

---

 📌 Business Recommendations

Based on the dashboard analysis:

- Improve supplier performance monitoring.
- Optimize delivery routes to reduce late shipments.
- Implement demand forecasting for inventory planning.
- Increase investment in high-margin product categories.
- Reduce damaged goods through improved handling and quality control.
- Expand operations in high-performing regions.
